_S_SCENE VIII. A plain near Actium.
_D_Enter OCTAVIUS CAESAR, and TAURUS, with his army, marching
_R_OCTAVIUS CAESAR
Taurus!
_R_TAURUS
My lord?
_R_OCTAVIUS CAESAR
Strike not by land; keep whole: provoke not battle,
Till we have done at sea. Do not exceed
The prescript of this scroll: our fortune lies
Upon this jump.
_D_Exeunt
