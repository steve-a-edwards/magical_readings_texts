_S_SCENE I. A desert place.
_D_Thunder and lightning. Enter three Witches
_R_First Witch
When shall we three meet again
In thunder, lightning, or in rain?
_R_Second Witch
When the hurlyburly's done,
When the battle's lost and won.
_R_Third Witch
That will be ere the set of sun.
_R_First Witch
Where the place?
_R_Second Witch
Upon the heath.
_R_Third Witch
There to meet with Macbeth.
_R_First Witch
I come, Graymalkin!
_R_Second Witch
Paddock calls.
_R_Third Witch
Anon.
_R_ALL
Fair is foul, and foul is fair:
Hover through the fog and filthy air.
_D_Exeunt
